## PART 1: THE BUSINESS IN ONE PAGE

### What We're Building

An **AI-powered intelligence service** that transforms public planning commission agendas into actionable business opportunities for construction professionals.

**Not:** A report service or data dump  
**Is:** An AI deal scout that lives in their workflow‚Äîfinds opportunities, ranks by fit, provides contacts, and pre-writes outreach

### The Core Value Proposition

We give dirt contractors and GCs a **72-hour head start** on every opportunity by:
1. **Automatically ingesting** planning agendas from target jurisdictions (weekly/daily)
2. **Extracting key entities** (owners, developers, engineers, architects, GCs) using AI
3. **Finding contact information** (phone, email, LinkedIn) from public and enriched sources
4. **Ranking opportunities by fit** to each client's specific business (past projects, sweet spot, capacity)
5. **Delivering actionable intel** via email brief + web portal with one-tap actions (call, email, calendar)
6. **Learning from behavior** to improve recommendations over time

### Why This Beats "Just Reading Agendas"

**The Manual Way (Current State):**
- Contractor spends 6-8 hours/week reading agenda PDFs
- Manually Googles entity names for contact info
- Writes outreach emails from scratch
- Misses 60%+ of opportunities (sporadic checking)
- Everyone does this (no competitive advantage)

**The Plan Way (Our Solution):**
- AI reads every agenda automatically (never miss anything)
- Entities and contacts extracted and enriched (zero manual work)
- Ranked by fit to YOUR business (not generic)
- Pre-written personalized outreach (in your voice, one-tap send)
- One-tap actions: call, email, add to calendar
- **Result: 72-hour head start, zero cognitive load**

### The "Secret Sauce" Story (For Skeptical Executives)

**Question:** "If everyone subscribes, isn't the advantage gone?"

**Answer (Three-Part):**

1. **Same data ‚â† same action**
   - We compress time from "agenda published" to "owner called" from 5-7 days to <24 hours
   - Competitor: reads agenda ‚Üí Googles contacts ‚Üí writes email ‚Üí calls days later
   - Plan subscriber: gets alert ‚Üí taps "call with script" ‚Üí connects in 2 minutes

2. **Personalization creates private advantages**
   - Your fit scores are different than your competitor's (based on YOUR past projects)
   - Your relationship history surfaces unique opportunities (AI knows you worked with this engineer before)
   - The system learns YOUR sweet spot, not a generic profile

3. **We integrate into workflow = consistency advantage**
   - Competitor checks portal when they remember (sporadic, misses opportunities)
   - Plan subscriber: automatic morning brief + calendar reminders + follow-up automation
   - They NEVER miss an opportunity; competitor misses 60%+

**Closing Line:**  
*"Think of it this way: everyone can buy a gym membership, but only some people get in shape. We're not just giving you the gym‚Äîwe're giving you a personal trainer who lives in your phone, knows exactly what workout you need each day, and literally books your appointments."*
