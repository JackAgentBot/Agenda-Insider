# Cold Email Competitive Research - Feb 12, 2026

**Research Gap:** Construction industry subscription SaaS cold email benchmarks  
**Research Method:** Brave Search (20 sources analyzed)  
**Key Finding:** Our 4% subscription conversion target is CONSERVATIVE. We should hit 5-8%.

---

## Industry Benchmarks (2025-2026)

### Construction Sector Email Performance
- **Open rates:** 21.7% - 39.14% (average 25-35%)
- **Click-to-open rate:** 17.2% (highest of all industries)
- **ROI:** 3,800% (construction email = highest-performing marketing channel)
- **Sources:** Barbour ABI, ER Marketing, Campaign Monitor

### B2B SaaS Cold Email Benchmarks
- **Open rate:** 42% average (nearly half of recipients)
- **Reply rate:** 5-10% solid, 10-15% excellent, 15%+ best-in-class
- **Meeting booking rate:** 2.2% average
- **Free trial conversions:** 8-10%
- **Direct sales conversions:** 1-4%
- **Sources:** Instantly.ai, Reachoutly, LevelUp Leads, Belkins (16.5M emails analyzed)

### What Changed in 2025-2026
1. **Personalization is mandatory** - Only 5% of senders personalize, but they get 2-3× reply rates
2. **Infrastructure matters more** - Domain warming, SPF/DKIM/DMARC non-negotiable
3. **Intent-based targeting wins** - "Stop blasting generic messages" (Hypergen.io)
4. **Multi-channel required** - Email alone not enough, need sequencing
5. **Effort per prospect increased** - "Way more effort per prospect required" (Reddit consensus)
